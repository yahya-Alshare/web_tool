What is CBM

The meaning of CBM is known in English as Cubic Meter, and in Arabic it is called the cubic meter, which is the product of multiplying length by width by height. This unit of measurement is used in shipping and clearance companies. When you ship products overseas by container (container), you calculate the packaging size (carton ) in cubic meters, and when you know the size of your goods, you will be able to choose the appropriate container for it, but if the size of your goods is small, i.e. less than the size of the smallest type of containers, then you must go to partial shipping and in partial shipping, you will be charged based on the size of your goods On CBM shipping, the clearance company will tell you, for example, that they charge $100 for each CBM, and through the cubic meter calculation program, which is also called the CBM calculator, it will help you calculate the total costs of shipping your goods.

CBM calculation method

With the prosperity of international trade, the importer, exporter, shipping and clearance companies constantly need to carry out the CBM calculation process and know how to calculate the CBM. Therefore, today we have found for you on our site an easy and fast calculation mechanism. First, you choose the appropriate unit of measurement for you, whether A centimeter or an inch, and then you enter the length of the carton, then its width, then its height, and at the end you enter the number, that is, the number of cartons you have, and the site will give you how many cubic meters your goods are equivalent to, that is, how many CBM you need in order to ship them, as the size of your goods may be small And you do not need a full container, so you can ship partially, and the size of the goods may be huge, so you need a large container or two small containers, and here are the container sizes commonly used in shipping companies.

Here is an applied example of how to calculate the cubic meter for shipping. For example, I have 120 cartons of standard size, and the size of one carton is 50 cm long and 100 cm wide. And a height of 50 cm. I provide the program on this page at the top with the size of one carton, then enter the number in the quantity field, and then press the Calculate button, and the result will be 30 CBM.
