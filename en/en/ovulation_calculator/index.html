How does a woman get pregnant

Pregnancy occurs in a woman when a man's sperm fertilizes a mature egg in a woman that comes out of a woman's ovary once a month. It is known that this egg lives for a very short period of 12 to 24 hours. He has sexual intercourse with the woman during this period known as the ovulation period, and intercourse can also be avoided during this period to regulate birth naturally without relying on any medical means.

How to calculate ovulation days

As we mentioned earlier, the ovulation period lasts from 12 to 24 hours, and this period cannot be determined with extreme accuracy, but in our approved calculation, the ovulation period is 14 days before the start of a woman's menstrual period. Therefore, in the Gregorian or Hijri ovulation calculator, we By taking the length of the menstrual cycle, i.e. (the period between menstruation and the next), then we add this period to the date of the first day of the last menstrual period, and then remove two weeks from the date, which is expected to be the ovulation period, and then two days before and two days after that date are taken to make an approximate field Around the ovulation period, it is called the days of ovulation or the days of ovulation, and thus we will have five days, which are the days of ovulation, and in these five days there will be 12 to 24 hours in which the egg is mature and ready for fertilization by the man’s sperm, and the woman who wants to become pregnant must be keen to have sexual intercourse within a period of days ovulation.
