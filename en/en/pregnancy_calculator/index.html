Pregnancy Calculator

The accurate electronically programmed pregnancy calculator shows how to calculate the correct pregnancy (calculating the gestational age or calculating the age of the fetus) for women who are waiting for their new baby, and this is done by entering the date of the first day of the woman’s last menstrual cycle. It will help the pregnant woman to track her pregnancy accurately to maintain her health and the health of her fetus, and among the information provided by the pregnancy and childbirth calculator is the expected date of fertilization and the expected date of delivery, in addition to the current pregnancy period in detail so that the pregnancy period that has passed so far, which is also called Gestational age, in addition to the current month of pregnancy, the week of the current pregnancy, and the number of days of pregnancy until Today's date.

Also, light is highlighted on the remaining pregnancy period, so that how much is left until the exact date of birth, and how much is left until the date of birth in terms of the number of months, also the number of weeks, and finally the number of days. The seasons of the year will be the date of birth, such as summer or spring, and any day of the week, for example, Sunday or Thursday, etc. At the end of the report, the horoscope of your newborn will be displayed.

Calculate date of birth

A woman who is waiting for her new birth asks how do I calculate my due date or how do I calculate my pregnancy in an easy and simple way. For this, we are always working here to develop and improve the process of calculating the due date with very high accuracy, or as it is called calculating the birth date by the Gregorian date or by the Hijri date, and the normal date of birth is usually After nine months of pregnancy, and according to the pregnancy and childbirth calculator presented to you, nine months and seven days are added to the entered date (the date of the first day of the last menstrual period) to obtain the expected date of birth, and thus the process of calculating the date of birth has been completed successfully, of course this is a Determine the expected date of birth, not the inevitable date of birth, but at this date the pregnancy will have been completed by nine full months, and the woman must give birth to her new baby, whether a natural birth or a cesarean section.

Calculate pregnancy by weeks

Most pregnant women prefer to track the stages of pregnancy and the growth of the fetus by weeks, so we offer a machine pregnancy calculator by weeks Because the health and medical information about the fetus is usually divided into weeks in the first week, the second week, the seventh week, and so on, and to make it easier for the pregnant woman to know the week of her current pregnancy, we display within the accurate pregnancy calculator the number of weeks of the current pregnancy and the number of weeks of pregnancy remaining, and thus the process of calculating the pregnancy By weeks, the woman has been provided with everything she needs about the weeks of her pregnancy without any need to perform other calculations.

Pregnancy calculator by months

The program for calculating pregnancy in electronic months is very important because the most reliable indicator of the pregnancy period is the month of pregnancy. The pregnancy calculator in months provides a very accurate breakdown of the number of months of pregnancy in the forty weeks and in the three basic stages. This detail includes the current month of pregnancy in addition to the number of months remaining until the date of birth. In order to ensure that the exact correct pregnancy is calculated, the pregnancy calculator in the Gregorian or Hijri months displays the number of days that have passed in the current month of pregnancy so that the pregnant woman has a complete picture of the number of days remaining for the end of the month of pregnancy in which she is going through.

Hijri pregnancy calculator

The accurate pregnancy calculator, or what is known as the birth calculator, provides the correct pregnancy calculation method from the last period in the Hijri date, because some Arab users in some Arab countries use the Hijri date in their daily lives. Therefore, our system allows Hijri pregnancy calculator Accurately, and provides the pregnant woman with the expected date of fertilization and the expected date of birth in Hijri, and thus the woman does not have to convert the date before performing the calculation, since our system supports the Hijri date with high accuracy and flexibility without the need to rely on external sources to convert the date.
